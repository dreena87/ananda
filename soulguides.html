ANANDA "BLISS" KENBOYA

Bliss is the ”Mid-Wife” for The Inner Soul Vibration. She is a Licensed Practitioner with Centers for Spiritual Living where she provides 1:1 prayer and counseling sessions for congregants and clients. Her role as leader of The Inner Soul Vibration is to witness and guide “receivers” to their own knowing of transformation and peace. Bliss believes in the transformative power of healing circles and uses her intuition as a guide to bring forth the healing sounds implemented during a circle. These healing sounds come to life through drumming, chants, singing, praying and reading. Bliss states, “No two circles are ever the same. We intend to create a personal and therapeutic experience for each "receiver" and the witnesses who have gathered in celebration and support. When we complete a circle we set an intention to impact how a receiver embraces the next leg of their journey.”

AL GIBSON

Al Gibson, CSLP, is a Licensed Spiritual Practitioner with Centers for Spiritual Living where he works with individuals providing guidance and clarity of life’s circumstances. His spiritual counseling focuses on the areas of finances, health, relationships and personal growth. He is the founder of Speak Life, Speak Love & Live: An organization that focuses on the healing power of love. His daily blog contains an inspirational message relating to love as a key component in bringing about social change to the world. He also leads workshops assisting participants in the development of a vision board. Vision boards are used by many businesses and individual as a tool for producing optimum results. Al is one of the Soul Guides for the Inner Soul Vibration organization led by Ananda “Bliss” Kenboya. Utilizing his skill of active listening teamed with compassion and intuition allows him to connect with Program Receivers.

ONA AFRAE

Ona Afrae is a native of the California Bay Area. As a holistic practitioner, she uses her natural abilities of intuition and empathy to guide her spiritual practice with her clients. Ona's expertise is in crisis management and counseling the effects of depression, hoarding, illness, addiction and PTSD. “Utilizing proven spiritual tools, we elevate the Truth about who we are within our individualized expressions of the Self. Through this process, we enter an energetic vibration that anchors our awareness in our essential perfection and oneness. My purpose is to be a pillar of support and guidance through this human experience. It is my belief that alone we struggle, yet together we can achieve great things”.
